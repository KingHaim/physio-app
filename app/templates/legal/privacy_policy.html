Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TRXCK ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our physiotherapy management platform, including all related services and features.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information (from clinicians or account holders)

  • Name, email, and contact details
  • Professional credentials and license numbers
  • Clinic/business details
  • Date of birth and demographic data

2.2 Patient Data (input by clinicians)

  • Patient names and contact info
  • Medical history and clinical notes
  • Treatment plans and assessments
  • Payment and billing information

Note: You are responsible for ensuring you have lawful grounds (e.g., patient consent) to process this data.

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP address and browser/device info
  • Usage data and activity logs
  • Cookies and session identifiers

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send important service-related communications
  • Analyze usage to improve features and user experience
  • Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you're located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on:

  • Contractual necessity (e.g., managing your account)
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., platform optimization)
  • Legal obligations (e.g., financial reporting)
  • Consent (e.g., marketing, optional analytics)

5. Data Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, including:

  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
  • HTTPS secure connections
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

6. Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your data. We only share it:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With trusted subprocessors (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers) under strict confidentiality
  • To comply with the law
  • To protect TRXCK's rights, users, or safety

A full list of subprocessors is available upon request.

We may process your data on servers located in the EU or abroad. Wherever your data goes, we ensure equivalent protection via:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions

  • Analyze performance

TRXCK is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.
