Data Processing Information

This section provides information about how we process your data in compliance with GDPR requirements.

Data Categories

  • Personal Data: Name, contact information, identification numbers
  • Health Data: Medical history, treatment records, diagnoses
  • Financial Data: Payment information, invoices, financial records
  • Usage Data: System logs, access records, preferences

Processing Purposes

  • Providing physiotherapy management services
  • Managing patient records and treatments
  • Processing payments and invoices
  • Maintaining system security and performance
  • Complying with legal obligations

Data Retention

We retain different types of data for different periods:

  • Patient records: 5 years after last treatment
  • Financial records: 6 years (legal requirement)
  • System logs: 1 year
  • User accounts: Until deletion is requested
